Our Mission

Our Vision

Our History

Founded in 2015 by Titus Kimari, Tito's Foundation began as a small initiative to support underprivileged youth in Bibirioni Ward through sports. Over the years, we've expanded our programs to include education support, healthcare initiatives, and community development projects, becoming a beacon of hope for the entire ward.
